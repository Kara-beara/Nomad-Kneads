Privacy Policy & SMS Terms

Last updated: May 2025

Nomad Kneads ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including how we handle SMS communications.

SMS Messaging & Opt-In

How you opt in: By providing your phone number through our booking form at nomadkneads.com, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Nomad Kneads. These messages may include appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and occasional promotional offers related to our massage and wellness services.

Message frequency will vary based on your appointment activity and any promotions we run. You will not receive unsolicited spam — only messages relevant to your bookings or wellness updates from Nomad Kneads.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any text message from us at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and will not receive further texts. You can also reply HELP for assistance or contact us directly at hello@nomadkneads.com.

We will never sell, share, or transfer your phone number to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Security

We maintain internal access controls, staff training, and security safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of customer data.

Data Sharing

  • Customer data is not shared with 3rd parties for promotional or marketing purposes.
  • Mobile opt-in and consent are never shared with anyone for any purpose. Any information sharing that may be mentioned elsewhere in this policy excludes mobile opt-in data.

Nomad Kneads Messaging Terms and Conditions

  1. The messaging program consists of general conversational messaging to answer questions and provide support to customers.
  2. You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text 'STOP' to the phone number from which you received messages. After you send the SMS message 'STOP' to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. If you want to join again, just sign up as you did the first time and we will start sending SMS messages to you again.

  6. If you have any questions regarding privacy, please read our privacy policy contained in the rest of this document/page.

We retain your contact and appointment information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with applicable laws. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.
